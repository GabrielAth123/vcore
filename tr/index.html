Posta İşleme, Tarama, İletme ve Yerel Telefon Numarası

Tüm mektuplarınızı ve paketlerinizi teslim alır, size hemen haber verir, önemli belgeleri tarar ve postalarınızı Yunanistan'ın herhangi bir yerine veya yurt dışına iletiriz. Ayrıca yerel bir Yunan iş telefonu numarası da alırsınız. Aramalar şirketinizin adıyla cevaplanabilir, mesajlar kaydedilebilir veya size iletilebilir, böylece önemli iletişimleri asla kaçırmazsınız.

Smart Call Center & Automated Call Handling

  1. 24/7 Call Answering: All incoming calls are answered automatically at any time, including outside business hours.
  2. Multilingual Communication: Calls can be handled in multiple languages based on caller selection or detection.
  3. Company-Specific Information: The system informs callers about your services, procedures and basic product information according to your company SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures).
  4. Appointment Booking & Requests: Clients can request appointments or service actions, which are automatically logged and forwarded to you or your team.
  5. Call Transcription & CRM Delivery: All conversations can be transcribed and sent to your email, CRM (Customer Relationship Management) or ticket system using automated workflows.
  6. Smart Escalation to Humans: When required, calls can be transferred to live agents or external partners based on rules and availability.
