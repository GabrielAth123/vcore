1. Data Controller

Data is stored on secure servers of Hetzner Online GmbH within the European Union and is processed exclusively for the provision of our services and compliance with our legal obligations.

To provide our services, we may share or process personal data through third-party service providers solely to the extent necessary for our business operations, the provision of our services, and compliance with applicable law.

International Transfers: Some service providers may process data outside the European Economic Area (EEA). In such cases, appropriate technical and contractual protection measures are taken in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use a Cookie Consent Banner to obtain your explicit consent. We use strictly necessary cookies for security and Google Analytics to improve our service. You can manage or revoke your consent at any time via the cookie settings on our website.

5. Your Rights

In accordance with the GDPR (EU Regulation 2016/679), you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, object to processing, and the right to data portability, where provided for by applicable law. To exercise your rights, you may contact us at contact@vcore.gr.

6. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide our services and to comply with tax, accounting, regulatory, and anti-money laundering (AML) obligations. Certain data may be retained for a longer period when required by Greek or European law. After the retention period expires, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Legal Basis for Processing

The processing of personal data is carried out in accordance with Article 6 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and is based, as the case may be, on the performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, the consent of the data subject, or the legitimate interest of VCORE in the safe and legal provision of its services.

8. Data Security

VCORE implements appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, loss, alteration, destruction, or illegal processing. These measures include secure servers, controlled access to data, encryption where required, and regular security audit procedures.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

VCORE reserves the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy whenever required for compliance, operational, or legislative reasons. The current applicable version is published on the company's website and becomes effective from the date of its publication.

10. Filing Complaints

If you believe that the processing of your personal data violates the applicable data protection legislation, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent Hellenic Data Protection Authority (HDPA) or the competent supervisory authority of your country of residence within the European Union.
